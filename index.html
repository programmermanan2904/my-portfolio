About Me

I'm Manan Jethwa, a 21-year-old B.Tech student at Swami Vivekanand College of Engineering, Indore. My journey in technology began with a deep curiosity to understand how data can drive decision-making, which has since blossomed into a passion for building impactful solutions with Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI. I have built a strong technical foundation in Python, HTML, CSS, Power BI, MySQL, and Data Analysis, which I use to bring complex projects to life.

I’ve applied these skills across a range of projects, from developing PlanItPerfect, a full-featured event management website for my minor project, to building my major project, MightBee—an ambitious AI-powered consulting platform designed to help individuals make better future choices. I also thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments, having conceptualized and developed Skill Swap, a skill-exchange platform, during a competitive hackathon.

Beyond building, I am committed to fostering tech communities. As a Founding Member and Non-Technical Team Lead of the Innovixus Club, I had the privilege of organizing "Pravah," a 24-hour hackathon. In leadership roles with the SV Tech Club and GDSC, I've designed creatives and hosted workshops on emerging AI tools like Hugging Face, Gamma, and Merlin AI. My enthusiasm for new frontiers also led me to conduct Web3 events on the Solana, BNB, and Aptos networks under the mentorship of industry seniors. To validate my skills, I've completed the Deloitte Task Simulation Program and am currently pursuing certification from Codveda.

When I’m not analyzing data or leading a project, I enjoy gaming, riding, and listening to music. I pride myself on being a good listener, an active collaborator, and someone who loves taking the initiative to turn an idea into reality.
